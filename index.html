How to Use

Complete Guide

Adding New Medicine

  • Enter medicine name (e.g., Napa, Antibiotic)
  • Select intake time (Morning, Noon, Night)
  • Select start date
  • Enter how many days to take
  • Click "Add Medicine" button with all information

Marking Medicine as Taken

Click the "Taken?" button next to each medicine to mark it. It will turn green and show "‚úÖ Taken".

Timer System

After marking medicine as taken, the timer for the next dose will start. When the timer ends, the medicine card will flash in red.

Finding Medicine

Type the medicine name in the search box to find it in real-time.

Data Backup

Click "Export Data" from the menu to download all your data.

Data Restore

Click "Import Data" from the menu to upload previous data.

App Sharing

Click "Share App" from the menu to see QR code and share the app.

App Installation

Click the "Install" button to install the app on your phone.
